Jake Calantas

How it

Started

I’ve been a creative for as long as I can remember, but things truly took shape in 9th grade when a senior in high school noticed my dedication and skills—and encouraged me to join our school publication.

From that point on, it all clicked. I built a habit of immersing myself in hours of YouTube tutorials, learning how to achieve eye-catching effects in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. That hunger to create never left.

How it's

Going

After graduating high school, joining student organizations was a top priority. I became a graphic artist for the Carig Campus Student Council and was soon absorbed by the University Student Council Federation in the same role.

For two years, I created designs for both—handling social media content and event visuals with a focus on clarity, strategy, and effective storytelling.

How it

Will Go

I’m committed to refining my craft and exploring new creative horizons—taking on bigger projects, collaborating with others, and going beyond student organizations.

From UI/UX to branding and motion graphics, I aim to merge strategy with storytelling and design with purpose. I don’t just want things to look good—I want them to resonate.

This is just the beginning.
